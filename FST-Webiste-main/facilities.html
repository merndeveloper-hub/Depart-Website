Meeting Rooms, Classrooms and Seminar Halls

Besides two faculty meeting rooms, the Department of Food Science & Technology has five spacious classrooms, two seminar/conference halls that are fully air-conditioned equipped with modern teaching tools and gadgets including whiteboards, overhead projectors, smart TVs and sound systems. The department acknowledges the generous support of Young’s Foods, UM Enterprises, United Kings and Dr. Essa Laboratories for renovating and modernizing our classrooms and seminar halls as Young’s Classroom, a big excellently furnished UM Hall, a state-of-the-start United King Seminar Hall and a beautiful Piece of Art Dr. Essa Lecture Hall respectively.

Laboratories

The Department Food Science & Technology embraces nine state-of-the-art laboratories equipped with all essential lab wares and sophisticated equipment. The array of laboratories include:

  • Food Engineering Lab
  • Food Chemistry Lab
  • Food Microbiology Lab
  • Food Quality Control Lab
  • Food Analysis Lab
  • Food Biochemistry & Post-Harvest Lab
  • Baking & Cereal Technology Lab
  • Food Packaging Lab
  • Oil & Fat Lab
  • Food Nutrition Lab
  • Muscle Food Lab
  • Analytical Lab
  • Sugar & Sweeteners Lab
  • Chocolate & Confectionery Lab
  • Dairy Lab
  • Beverage Lab
  • Food Fermentation Lab

The cluster of laboratories consists of sophisticated instruments, cutting edge technology for food analyses such as Gas Chromatograph (GC), High Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC), Atomic Absorption Assay, UV Monitored Fraction Collector, Cyroscope for detection of adulteration in dairy products, Protomate for dye binding protein analysis, Gel Permeation Chromatograph Technique, Kjeltec for Protein estimation, UV Spectrophotometer, Flouro Toxicity Meter, Flash Chromatograph, Gel Electrophoresis, Paper Electrophoresis, Alcometer etc. some major food processing units like Spray Drier, Drum Drier, Fluidized Bed Drier, Freeze Drier, Rotary Drier, Sun Drier, Tray Drier, Canning Machine, Bottling Machines, Pasteurizer, Trickle Filter, Mixer, Pin Mill, Ball Mill, Tablet Punching Machine, Tablet Coating Machine, Sieve Shaker, Modified Atmosphere Packaging, Shrink Packaging, Plastic Sealing, Wax Coating etc. Facilities are available for detection and estimation of major ingredients present in food and food products such as: Proteins, Fats, Carbohydrates, Dietary fibers, Ash, Moisture, Colors, Flavors, Preservatives, Vitamins, Antioxidants, toxic Materials e.g. Aflatoxin etc. Also present Enzymic assay, Biological Oxygen Demand (BOD), Chemical Oxygen Demand (COD), Dissolved Oxygen (DO), Self Controlled Minifermenter with all the accessories for product development. Assay for antibacterial and antifungal activity. Drop diffusate technique for food preservation, enzyme inhibition activity etc.

Seminar Library

Department’s Seminar Library has about 1000 books and research journals. We also have a digital section equipped with computers with high speed internet facility where students and researchers have direct access to thousands of eBooks and research journals through HEC Digital library.
